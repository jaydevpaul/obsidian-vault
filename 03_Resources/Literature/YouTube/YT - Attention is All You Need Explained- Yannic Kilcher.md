## 📝 Summary
The transformer replaces recurrence and convolutions entirely with self-attention. It enables massive parallelization during training and learns long-range dependencies more effectively than RNNs. The architecture is the foundation of every modern LLM.

## 🔑 Key Ideas
- Self-attention computes a weighted sum of all positions, where weights come from query-key dot products
- Multi-head attention lets the model attend to different representation subspaces simultaneously
- Positional encodings inject sequence order since attention itself is permutation-invariant
- Encoder-decoder structure: encoder builds representations, decoder generates autoregressively## 💬 Quotes / Timestamps
